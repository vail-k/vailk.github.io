Privacy Policy - Alphabet Boy

Last Updated: [Date]

Children's Privacy (COPPA Compliance)

Alphabet Boy is designed for children under 13 and complies with COPPA:

  • No Personal Information Collected: We do not collect names, email addresses, photos, or any personal data
  • No Location Data: We do not access or collect location information
  • No Device Identifiers: We do not collect device IDs or persistent identifiers
  • No Third-Party Sharing: We do not share any data with third parties
  • No Advertising: Our app contains no advertisements
  • Parental Gate: All purchases require parental verification through a math challenge
  • Local Storage Only: Game progress is stored only on your device

Data Collection

We collect NO personal information. The only anonymous data we may receive is:

  • Crash reports (anonymous, no user identification)
  • Purchase verification from Apple App Store

Parental Rights

Parents can:

  • Review any information related to their child
  • Request deletion of data
  • Refuse further collection
  • Contact us at: support@youremail.com
